UR2NLP

University of Rochester Under-Resourced NLP Lab

Advancing language technology for the world's under-resourced languages

About

The UR2NLP lab focuses on developing computational methods for languages with limited digital resources and training data. Our research spans multilingual language modeling, low-resource adaptation, and applications to language documentation and revitalization.
